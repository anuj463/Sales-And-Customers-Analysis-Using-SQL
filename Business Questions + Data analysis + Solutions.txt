1.How much total sales did the business generate?

2.How much sales did each customer make?

3.How many orders does each customer place?

4.Which product categories generate the most sales?

5.What is the average order value?

Solutions -

solution 1: 
          SELECT SUM(sales_amount)
          FROM orders;

solution 2:
          SELECT SUM(sales_amount),customer_id
          FROM orders
          GROUP BY customer_id;
solution 3:
          SELECT count(order_id),customer_id
          FROM orders
          GROUP BY customer_id;
solution 4:
          SELECT category, SUM(sales_amount)
          FROM orders
          GROUP BY category;
solution 5:
           SELECT AVG(sales_amount)
           FROM orders;
